# Market Analysis

## Use Case

Use this prompt to analyze a market, segment, product category, or business opportunity.

## Best For

- ChatGPT with browsing
- Claude
- Gemini
- Perplexity

## Prompt

```text
You are analyzing a market opportunity from a practical business and investment perspective.

Market:
[MARKET]

Context:
[CONTEXT]

Target customer:
[TARGET_CUSTOMER]

Constraints:
[CONSTRAINTS]

Return the analysis in this format:
1. Market summary
2. Customer segments and jobs-to-be-done
3. Demand drivers
4. Competitive landscape
5. Business model options
6. Distribution channels
7. Risks and barriers to entry
8. Key assumptions to validate
9. Recommended next steps

Separate facts from assumptions. If current data matters, cite sources or state what needs verification.
```

## Variables

| Placeholder | Description |
|---|---|
| `[MARKET]` | Market, segment, niche, or category to analyze |
| `[CONTEXT]` | Why the analysis is needed and how it will be used |
| `[TARGET_CUSTOMER]` | Customer type, buyer, user, or segment |
| `[CONSTRAINTS]` | Geography, budget, time horizon, business model, or exclusions |

## Example Usage

```text
Market:
AI tools for small accounting firms.

Context:
I am exploring whether this is a viable SaaS opportunity.

Target customer:
Small accounting firms with 5 to 50 employees.

Constraints:
Focus on English-speaking markets and practical go-to-market risks.
```

## Expected Output

A commercially useful market brief with customer segments, competition, risks, assumptions, and next steps.

## Notes

- Best results require current market data and competitor examples.
- Validate assumptions before using the output for investment or product decisions.
- For regulated markets, add legal and compliance constraints.
